# Product Overview

## What is CreatorFlow?

CreatorFlow is an AI-powered workflow automation platform built specifically for content creators. It automates repetitive tasks, centralizes multi-platform management, and uses AI to optimize content strategy.

## Core Problem

Content creators waste 60-70% of their time on repetitive administrative tasks instead of creating content:
- Manually posting across multiple platforms
- Tracking sponsorships and deliverables
- Responding to similar questions repeatedly
- Analyzing metrics from different platforms
- Managing collaboration requests

A creator earning $5,000/month wastes approximately $3,000 worth of time on admin tasks.

## Key Features

### Visual Workflow Builder
- Drag-and-drop interface for creating automation workflows
- Node-based architecture with triggers and actions
- Real-time execution monitoring

### AI Content Repurposing
Transform one piece of content into multiple platform-optimized formats:
- YouTube video → Twitter thread + Instagram carousel + LinkedIn post + Blog article
- Podcast episode → Show notes + Audiograms + Newsletters

### Platform Integrations
- **AI Models**: OpenAI GPT-4, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, DeepSeek
- **Communication**: Discord, Slack
- **Payment Tracking**: Stripe webhooks
- **Forms**: Google Forms triggers
- **Social Media**: YouTube, Twitter, Instagram (in progress)

### Smart Features
- Secure credential management with encryption
- Template marketplace for sharing workflows
- Real-time execution status and logs
- Automatic retries and error handling

## Target Audience

- YouTubers (500K-5M subscribers)
- Instagram Creators (100K-1M followers)
- TikTok Creators (500K-5M followers)
- Podcast Hosts (10K-100K listeners)
- Newsletter Writers (5K-50K subscribers)
- Content Creator Agencies

## Business Model

**Pricing Tiers:**
- **Free**: 100 executions/month, 3 workflows, 2 connections
- **Pro ($19/mo)**: 5,000 executions, unlimited workflows, AI repurposing (50/mo)
- **Business ($49/mo)**: 25,000 executions, team collaboration, AI repurposing (500/mo)
- **Agency ($199/mo)**: 100,000 executions, multi-client management, custom integrations

## Competitive Advantages

- Built specifically for content creators (not generic automation)
- AI-powered content repurposing unique to this platform
- Visual workflow builder (no coding required)
- Affordable pricing for individual creators
- Creator-specific templates and integrations
